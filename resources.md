# 📚 The AI PM Interview Resource Hub

*Every resource typed, annotated, dated, and license-noted. No bare link dumps. Capped per topic. Updated 2026-07.*

Legend: `📄 paper · 📘 docs · 🎬 video · 📰 article · 💻 repo · 📕 book`. Repos carry **stars + license** (as of 2026-07-01).

> [!NOTE]
> **No single repo covers AI PM end-to-end** — the honest answer is a hybrid. Broad PM curates (ProductHired) are the least AI-specific; AI code repos are the least PM-oriented. This hub links the best of both and tells you what each is for. Recommended prep split: **~25% new fixtures** (4-part eval rubric + Anthropic agent patterns + NIST AI RMF) · **~50% real AI-PM question drills** · **~25% CIRCLES + Decode and Conquer**.

## A. AI Technical Fluency for PMs

- 📰 [AI Product Manager Interview Questions](https://igotanoffer.com/en/advice/ai-product-manager-interview) — IGotAnOffer, May 2026. Canonical concepts + verbatim questions; the single densest source of real AI-PM prompts.
- 🎬 [Intro to Large Language Models](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=zjkBMFhNj_g) — Andrej Karpathy. The no-math tour of the generation loop.
- 📰 [What are LLMs and Why Do They Matter to PMs](https://www.productmanagementexercises.com/blog/what-are-large-language-models-pms/) — PM Exercises. PM-framed LLM primer.
- 📄 [What is RAG?](https://www.ibm.com/think/topics/retrieval-augmented-generation) — IBM. Official explainer + the "answer changes when the world changes → RAG" test.
- 📘 [Building Effective AI Agents](https://www.anthropic.com/research/building-effective-agents) — Anthropic, Dec 2024. The workflow-vs-agent taxonomy every agent round expects. *License: Anthropic docs — link + commentary.*
- 📘 [Choose a Design Pattern for Agentic AI](https://docs.cloud.google.com/architecture/choose-design-pattern-agentic-ai-system) — Google Cloud, May 2026. Agentic-pattern decision guidance.
- 💻 [LLM Interview Questions Hub](https://github.com/KalyanKS-NLP/LLM-Interview-Questions-and-Answers-Hub) — KalyanKS-NLP. Depth on LLM concepts. *License: Apache-2.0 (inferred) · ~971★.*
- 💻 [AI Engineering Interview Questions](https://github.com/amitshekhariitbhu/ai-engineering-interview-questions) — amitshekhariitbhu. Concept drills. *License: MIT · ~1.9k★.*
